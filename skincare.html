Skincare Basics

Start With Simple, Daily Habits

Understanding the basics of skincare can make everything feel much easier. You do not need a long routine or expensive products. The goal is to keep your skin clean, protected, and hydrated. Below are the three main steps that almost every routine follows.

1. Cleanser

A gentle cleanser removes dirt, oil, and makeup without stripping your skin.

2. Moisturizer

Helps your skin stay soft, healthy, and balanced throughout the day.

3. Sunscreen

The most important step. Sunscreen protects your skin from sun damage and early aging.
