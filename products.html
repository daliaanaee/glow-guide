Product Tips

Choosing Skincare Products That Actually Work

With so many products on the market, it’s easy to spend money on things that are not right for your skin. Below is a simple comparison table that shows different products, what they do, who they are best for, and their general price range.

Product Name Purpose Best For Price Range
CeraVe Gentle Cleanser Removes dirt without stripping skin Normal to dry skin $10 - $15
The Ordinary Niacinamide Serum Reduces redness and improves texture All skin types $6 - $10
La Roche Posay Sunscreen SPF 50 Sun protection and anti-aging All skin types $25 - $35
Neutrogena Hydro Boost Gel Cream Lightweight hydration Oily or combo skin $15 - $20

How to Choose the Right Products

• Look for gentle, fragrance-free options if your skin is sensitive.
• Avoid buying too many “trending” products at once. Start slow.
• Always add sunscreen, no matter your skin type.
• Focus on your skin’s needs, not what influencers promote.

Understanding what each product does can help you build a routine that fits your skin and your budget.
